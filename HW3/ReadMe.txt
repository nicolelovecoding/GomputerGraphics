
Key Board Manipulation Discription:

'd' show/hide control points

's' show/hide surface

'f' show/hide face model

'e' show/hide eyes

't' show/hide texture

'm' raycasting

'l' activate/stop the animation of the smiley face

'r' reset everthing in this scene.

'c' turn on/turn off the camera.

'p' move/stop moving the top most control point of the cap.

Arrow keys:

when 'c' is pressed, the arrow keys are to move the camera on a sphere of radius 10.

when 'p' is pressed, the up and down keys are to move the top most control points up and down.
